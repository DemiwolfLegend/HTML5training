Thank you for joining TJ

We built ThoughtJumper with the aim to revolutionize exploration and sharing of meaningful content.

The internet is an infinitely huge library of information that keeps growing substantially every second. Information on anything you want to know/learn is available for free. Finding relevant information on the other hand is taxing and does not always yield accurate results. TJ aims to help you navigate, record and share information that is meaningful to you efficiently with an approach that seems so natural yet has never been available before.
